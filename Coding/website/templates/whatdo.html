What We Do

Typology of intervention areas:

Improvement of the Shareholder and Corporate Governance models

  • Consultation with shareholders and construction / reinforcement of common principles and values
  • Preparing or reviewing Family Protocols
  • Formalization of key Shareholder Governance bodies (Family Council, others) and their relationship with the Corporate Governance bodies
  • Support in resolving conflicts and strengthening cohesion within the shareholder forum
  • Alignment of the Corporate Governance model with the best business management conditions

Alignment of the Business Strategy with the purpose and values of the Shareholders

  • Alignment of the business strategic development vision
  • Research of national and international strategic partnerships, business and (or) provision of strategic services
  • Consistency assessment of global Strategic Plans or specific areas

Next generation onboarding and development programs

  • Independent assessment of the potential of next generation members and their future prospects
  • Conception of integration programs in the business - participation in the Family Council, internships ...
  • Conception of career plans given the potential and interest in occupying top positions in the business
  • Definition of succession programs - selection, preparation and leadership transition

Organizational development and people management

  • Design and management of far-reaching programs to transform organizational models
  • Assessing the performance of organizational models and recommending improvements for greater effectiveness, efficiency and alignment with business objectives
  • Leadership development via mentoring and counseling, often linked to the succession model
  • Assessments by Senior Management and Senior Management and recommendation of actions to be considered
